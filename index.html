The Projects

Perturb-Seq

In this Single Cell CRISPR Screen mutliomics project 4 assay types were analyzed (RNA, Donor, tetramers and proteins), to understand transcription factors knocking out affect in biology and function of dsyfunctional T Cells (exhausted cells). Further define temporal signatures for stages of memory  vs. exhausted cell differentiation and cell heterogeneity.

Epigenetic remodelling of dysfunctional T Cells treated with DNA-PK inhibitor

Bulk ATAC-Seq was analyzed to understanding epigenetic landscape of exhausted T Cell and to understand mechanism of action DNA-PK inhibitor in epigenetic remodelling of this exhausted cells in restoration functional capabilities in this cells. The findings were further validated by analyzing RNA-Seq data.

Differential expression of microRNA in SARS-Cov-2 patients

Differential miRNA expression patterns were analyzed to comparing SARS-CoV-2 infected samples with mock controls. Further changes in expression pattern were analyzed across different time points (24h and 72h).

Cell heterogeneity and Cell Differentiation in Development Heart tissue

10x Visium developmental heart tissue was analyzed using BASS, Scanpy, scType for cell type spatial distribution and identification.

PD inhibitor clinical response in patients

This project implements a neural network that combines RNA expression data with clinical biomarkers to predict patient response to cancer treatment. The model uses a dual-branch architecture that processes genomic and clinical data separately before fusion for final prediction.
